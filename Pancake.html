Pancake Recipe

Pancake

A pancake is a flat, round breakfast dish made from a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder. It's cooked on a griddle and enjoyed with toppings like syrup, fruits, or even savory ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Prepare Your Pan
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients
  3. Mix Wet Ingredients
  4. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients
  5. Cook the Pancakes
  6. Serve
